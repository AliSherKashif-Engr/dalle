How Outpainting Works in DALL-E
Just like with inpainting, for now, you cannot directly outpaint existing images with the DALL-E integrated in ChatGPT. You can do it on a separate DALL-E platform on the openart.ai website.
Nonetheless, here's a step-by-step guide to outpainting with DALL-E: 1. Generate an image with the ChatGPT version of DALL-E or upload it to the openart.ai platform. 2. Choose the direction you want to expand – top, bottom, left, or right. 3. Specify how much you want to extend the image. 4. Provide a prompt describing what you want in the new area. 5. DALL-E will generate the extended part, seamlessly blending it with the original image.

Tips for Seamless Outpainting
To continue achieving the best results with outpainting consistently, keep these tips in mind: 1. Use similar color palettes and lighting conditions 2. Maintain the same art style or photographic quality 3. Consider perspective and scale when adding new elements 4. Ensure logical continuity with the original scene 5. The more detailed your prompts are, the better DALL-E can match your vision.
