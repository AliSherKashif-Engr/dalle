What is Copyright?
So, what exactly is copyright? Think of it as a powerful shield for creators! It's a form of protection that grants exclusive rights to those who craft original works, whether they're literary, dramatic, musical, or artistic.
This means that as a creator, you have the freedom to use, distribute, and modify your work without worry! Here are some practical steps to ensure your AI-generated images comply with copyright laws: - Research and understand the copyright status of any reference images or data used. - Avoid using copyrighted materials without permission. - Provide attribution where applicable. - Use royalty-free or public domain resources whenever possible. - Consult legal advice if unsure about the copyright status of your work.
