The Basics of Color Theory
Let's kick things off with the essentials! The color wheel is your best friend - it's a visual delight that showcases colors arranged by their relationships. Primary colors (red, blue, yellow) are your building blocks, while secondary colors (green, orange, purple) spring to life when you mix those primaries. And let's not forget tertiary colors, which are crafted by blending primary and secondary colors!
Complementary colors sit opposite each other on the wheel and create a stunning contrast that makes each hue pop with vibrancy! On the other hand, analogous colors, nestled next to each other, create a beautiful harmony. And for those looking for balance and flair, triadic colors are evenly spaced around the wheel, offering a lively and balanced palette.
